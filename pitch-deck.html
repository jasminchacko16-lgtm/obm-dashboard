Our Methodology

The SPACE Model

S

Scope

Define the organizational context, goals, and constraints with stakeholders

➜
P

Pinpoint

Identify specific, observable, measurable target behaviors

➜
A

Assess

Collect baseline data and conduct functional assessments (ABC)

➜
C

Change

Implement targeted interventions and track behavior data continuously

➜
E

Embed

Transfer ownership, build internal systems, ensure sustainability
